Welding

I took up welding because I wanted a utility trailer. I looked at several of them and thought...I bet I could build one just as good or better. So I did. I took a welding class at a local high school, bought the tools, and got to work. It was challenging at times when things didn't go as smoothly as planned, but the result turned out great. I have used the trailer on several trips and it is just what I wanted!
